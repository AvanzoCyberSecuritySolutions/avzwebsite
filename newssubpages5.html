Kerala Police Facebook Abuse Case – Legal Action Taken

A case has been registered after an abusive and obscene comment was posted under an official Kerala Police Facebook page.

  • The incident highlights that online abuse is a punishable offence.
  • Social media is a public space — comments and posts can lead to legal action.
  • Freedom of speech does not mean freedom to abuse.
Action Taken:
  • The Cyber Police traced the account and registered a case under relevant legal sections.
Think before you comment. Stay respectful. Stay legal.
